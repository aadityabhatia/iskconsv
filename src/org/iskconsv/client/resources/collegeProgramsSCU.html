ISKCON Silicon Valley in collaboration with Hindu Society Club of Santa Clara University hosts actively host weekly programs within the university campus. Hindu Society Club(HSC) at Santa Clara University is a body of students and faculty who actively host and participate in these events. Along with students, faculty or Staff of Santa Clara University, we invite everyone irrespective ofÂ their faith or cultural to participate in these events.

Following weekly programs offer a forum for philosophical and cultural discussions, mantra meditation, kirtan and vegetarian cooking classes.

Vedic Cooking Techniques

The program begins with a brief talk by one of the guest speakers on a spiritual topic. We have many guest speakers who are expert on various areas which include Ayurved-Science of Cooking, Yoga for Good Health, Mantra Meditation for calming the mind, Timeless wisdom from Bhagavad Gita, Congregational participation in sweet indian traditional music, Yoga exercises like pranayama and asanas, Leadership topics from epic Ramayan and Mahabharat etc. After the talk, there is a demonstration of one of the savouries followed by delicious dinner. The program culminates as a forum for meeting new people and making new friends. An ideal setting to spend our weekend time in releasing all the stress accumulated over the week.

Philosophical Discussions

In this program, we take a verse from Bhagavad-Gita (an anciet timeless wisdom) and discuss its personal application in our day to day lives. In history, there have been great leaders like Mahatma Gandi, Henry Thoreau who have extolled the glories of this ancient book of wisdom.

This is a very nice forum to discuss many personal impediments and seek a holistic solution. We also get to understand more deeper aspects of our existence, our relationship with nature and other living entities. The program is intended to provide a vision and a direction for our lives.
