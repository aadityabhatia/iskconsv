Pursue spiritual life, while earning your college degree.

Experience dynamic lecture, group discussion, guided meditations and kirtans, multimedia presentations and a framework to personally cultivate strong spiritual habits.

Bhakti Yoga Club at SJSU offers systematic training in personality development, character build up, science and spirituality, Meditation techniques, Leadership and management skills for the overall personality development of student and youth community. We invite everyone to participate in following ongoing programs at san jose state university:

Weekly Bhagavad Gita Discussion

We invite everyone to weekly discussions on practical application of Vedic wisdom of Bhagavad Gita followed by a delicious Vegetarian feast. These discussion will give us valuable insight into most fundamental questions of Life such as:
  • What is my real self?
  • Where did I come from?
  • Where do I go after death?
  • What is the aim of my life?
In addition, these discussions will give us a way to uproot and RISE above: Stress, Depression, Lust and Anger. It helps us increase confidence, concentration and enhance our personality.

Morning Mantra Meditation and Prayers

Along with weekly seminars, we also have everyday morning program for interested students.

Morning program consists of:
  • Mantra meditation
  • Prayers
  • Studies of scriptures
  • Breakfast

Discover Your Self Course

DYS Course consists of six presentations which clears major doubts of modern intellectual youth about God, Soul, and Scriptures such as Bhagavad Gita. The whole training awakens one’s dormant capabilities and brings out the hidden potentials and values from the heart towards one and all.
  • Can a scientist believe in God?
  • Getting Eyes to See God
  • Vedic Literatures – A Privilige to humanity
  • Science of Soul
  • Substance and Shadow
  • If there is one God, why so many religions?

Leadership skills

We also train the students to become enlightened principle-centered cheerful leaders by conducting regular seminars on developing competence and skill enhancement such as:
  • Time Management
  • Self Management
  • Proactive Leadership
  • Organizational Behavior
  • Communication Skills
  • Yoga for the Modern Age
  • Anger Management
  • Stress Management
  • Positive Thinking
  • Power of Habits

Student C.A.R.E. (Counseling, Assistance, Resources, and Education)

We offers personal counseling and assistance to overcome stress, anxiety, fear and anger, and to handle difficult circumstances of life in a matured way.

Practical assistance includes equipping the students with education and resources which broadly synthesize wisdom from the lives of great personalities and scriptures.  It focus on universal principles relevant to people of all caste, creed, color, nationality, section etc.
